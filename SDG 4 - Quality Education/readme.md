##   Quality Education - Sustainable Development Goal (SDG 4)

> **Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.**

### Focus Areas
- Universal access to primary & secondary education  
- Affordable technical, vocational, and higher education  
- Eliminating gender disparities in education  
- Improving learning outcomes and teacher training  

### Why It Matters
Quality education empowers individuals, reduces inequality, and drives sustainable development.
